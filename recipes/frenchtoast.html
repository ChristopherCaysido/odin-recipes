French Toast

Ingredients

  • 6 Thick Slices of Bread
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 2/3 cup of Milk
  • Cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp. granulated sugar

Steps:

  1. In a shallow bowl combine the eggs, milk, cinnamon and granulated sugar
  2. Whisk the mixture until the egg are combined with no visible yolk and white
  3. Heat up your non-stick pan to medium-high and add in the unsalted butter
  4. Dip your bread into the custard mixture and let is soak on both sides
  5. Fry your soaked bread in the pan and let it fry for 3 minutes on each side
  6. Serve with maple syrup and enjoy
