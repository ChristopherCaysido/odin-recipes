Peanut Noodles

This recipe is a quick and easy 10 minute peanut sauce noodle that will surely satisfy your cravings

Ingredients

  • Fresh Noodles / Instant Ramen Noodles
  • 1 Tbsp. Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tbsp. Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp. Oyster Sauce
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • 1 Tsp. Sugar
  • A handful of sliced spring Onions (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Boil your fresh or instant ramen noodles and boil until done with the recommend time for cooking. While the noodles are cooking we can prepare the other ingredients
  2. In a bowl combine the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, peanut butter and sugar, grate the clove of garlic and mix together
  3. After the noodles are done cooking save a little bit of noodle water to help the sauce emulsify and put it in the bowl
  4. Drain the noodles and mix the peanut butter sauce until evenly mixed
  5. Optional: add the spring onions for garnish
