Chicken Oyakodon

Ingredients

  • Sliced Chicken Thighs
  • Half an onion (Sliced in wedges)
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/4th cup dashi stock / dashi powder stock
  • 1 tbsp. Soysauce
  • 1 tbsp. Mirin
  • 1 tbsp. granulated sugar
  • Optional: Mitsuba Parsley / Regular Parsley

Steps:

  1. In a frying pan combine the dashi stock, soysauce, mirin and sugar, heat the frying pan and add the sliced onions and let it simmer
  2. After the onions are slightly translucent add in the chicken thighs and cover
  3. After the 4 minutes flip the chicken thighs, simmer again for 3 minutes
  4. Set the heat to high and add in the egg mixture
  5. After 10 seconds kill the heat and transfer to a bowl
  6. Serve with maple syrup and enjoy
